Hello Friends
Email, Blog

Developer/Designer

CSS/HTML, Bootstrap,
JavaScript, jQuery

Hello Friends started as a passion project during the pandemic to brighten the spirits of friends and family. The project is a curation of emails containing positive content from around the Web, each thoughtfully designed under different themes.

I built, designed and wrote each of these emails, injecting the HTML during into Gmail to avoid services like Mail Chimp or Constant Contact.

The header of each email was designed or from a design modified by me to fit the theme.

People started sharing and the project organically grew to 500+ subscribers so I build an archive to give access to older emails and allow people to share, subscribe and make submissions.

The website features each of the emails with their original designs.

I'm continuing with this weekly.
